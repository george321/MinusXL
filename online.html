Dhmiourgia neou fyllou ergasias
 
Gia na dhmiourghseis ena neo fyllo ergasias patas to koumpi Arxeio>Neo h CTRL+N kai anoigei ena neo parathyro sto opoio dineis to onoma kai tis diastaseis tou neou fyllou kai patas to koumpi dhmiourgia. Diaforetika boreis na pathseis to koumpi deufault kai tha dwthoun prokathorismenes times..
 
 
Apothhkefsh tou fyllou ergasias
Gia na apothhkefseis to trexwn fyllo ergasias patas to koumpi Arxeio>Apothhkefsh h CTRL+S kai anoigei ena neo parathyro sto opoio epielgeis pou theleis na apothhkeftei to arxeio sou.
 
Diagrafh tou fyllou ergasias
Gia na diagrapseis to trexwn fyllo ergasias patas to koumpi Arxeio>Diagrafh Fyllou h CTRL+D kai to trexwn fyllo ergasias diagrafete.
 
Fortwsh arxeiou CSV
Gia na fortwseis ena arxeio CSV patas to koumpi Arxeio>Anoigma h CTRL+O kai anoigei ena neo parathyro sto opoio epielgeis to arxeio pou theleis na fortwseis.
 
Eisagwgh synarthshs
Gia na eisageis mia synarthsh epilegeis ena keli kai meta patas to koumpi Synarthsh sto parathyro pou emfanizetai epilegeis mia synarthsh kai patas OK epeita sto neo parathyro epilegeis ta kelia pou theleis gia thn synarthsh.
An theleis na eisageis ena ena ta kelia tote eisageis ta kelia sto pedio Kelia me thn ekshs morfh,estw oti ta kelia A1,A2,D3  tote apla grafeis ta kelia xwrizwntas ta me «,».Dhladh A1,A2,D3  .
An theleis na eisageis ena oloklhrh grammh h sthlh tote eisageis ta kelia sto pedio Synolo grammwn h Sthlwn me thn ekshs morfh,estw oti ta kelia apo A1 ews D1  tote apla grafeis to arxiko keli kai to teliko keli xwrizwntas to me «:».Dhladh A1:D1  .
 
Eisagwgh grafhmatos
Gia na eisageis ena grafhma patas to koumpi Graph kai epilegeis ton typo tou grafhmatos pou theleis. Meta sto  parathyro pou tha emfanistei sto pedio Onoma Grafhmatos dineis enan titlo, sto pedio Onomata aksona X dineis ta onomata twn timwn pou anaparistas , sto pedio Onoma aksona Y  dineis to onoma tou typou twn timwn (PX Vathmoi Kelsiou) sto pedio Kathgories dineis ta onomata twn kathgoriwn gia paradeigma Ypshloterh kai Xamhloterh THermokrasia.
Kai sta pedia Memonomena kelia h Synolo Keliwn eisageis ta kelia gia na parei to programma tis times.
